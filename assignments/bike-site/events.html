Cycling Events in Montana

Every year, the Mountain Spoke team participates in events and sponsors riders with a passion to experience the American wilderness at its finest.

Cycle the Continental Divide

The Continental Divide is the longest off-pavement route in the world; a call of the wild for biking enthusiasts from around the world. Crisscrossing the Continental Divide from north to south, bikers gather once a year for a life-changing ride across the country.

The Continental Divide route is geographically divided into five regions. Each of these regions is diverse, making for a truly incredible scenic experience. This amazing route offers diversity in many ways; the landscape, riding conditions, points of interest, and folks along the way all make for an exciting adventure. Ride the Continental Divide with the Mountain Spoke team to experience the heart of America as it was meant to be seen!

The Butte 100 Race

With a title like the “Most Difficult Mountain Bike Race” in the country, the Butte 100 is a must for any serious cycling competitor. Race weekend offers three individual races of varying length and intensity: a 100 mile, 50 mile, and the Sorini 25. Each of these races take place atop the Continental Divide just outside of Butte, Montana.

The Butte 100 began as a small group of local biking enthusiasts racing on a gravel road, but it has grown to become a world-class competition attracting athletes from across the globe. draws the top cyclists in the nation right here to our proud state of Montana. This race is truly a sporting event to behold as mountain bikers take on extreme routes in the high-altitude mountains south of Butte. Join in this awe-inspiring race to the finish line.

Saturday July 28, 2018
Butte, Montana
