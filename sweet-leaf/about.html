The Sweet Leaf Story

Every year, the Mountain Spoke team participates in events and sponsors riders with a passion to experience the American wilderness at its finest.

Sally's Side

Growing up, my father always used to quote C.S.Lewis:

“You can never get a cup of tea large enough or a book long enough to suit me.”

I suppose I took those words to heart, because tea became a passion of mine (and books of course, but that's for another day!)

Joe and I set out to create signature blends that would reflect our vision, and we are proud to share our love of tea with our new home in Salt Lake City.

Joe's Side

Sweet Leaf started for me with a cup of tea in a stranger's home in Japan. The artistry and tradition that surrounded a simple cup of tea amazed me, and I was instantly hooked on the authenticity of the experience.

Most Americans don't have a huge sense of tradition when it comes to tea ceremonies, but I was determined to introduce some of the flavors and sights home with me. I returned to America after travelling abroad with a notebook filled with notes and ideas for the teas I wanted to blend. That's when I met Sally, and the Sweet Leaf name was born.
