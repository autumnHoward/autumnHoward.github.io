Mountain Spoke Adventure Tours

One of the most spectacular ways to experience Montana is on two wheels with a man-powered engine. From vast, sweeping plains to dramatic mountains and deep gorges, Montana puts the wild in wilderness. A Mountain Spoke trail guide will show you the adventure of a lifetime on some of our favorite local trails in the Bozeman area. Whether you are new to biking or a die-hard enthusiast, we offer guided trail rides for riders of all experience levels and all ages. Ride with us to find out why Montana is really called Big Sky Country.

Bozeman Town Tour

We’ll begin our town tour at the Mountain Spoke bike shop. Some sights you’ll enjoy include the Museum of the Rockies, cruising through the campus of Montana State University, and exploring some of the friendly neighborhoods that make Bozeman feel like home. We’ll wrap up the tour with a family-friendly picnic at Gallatin County Regional Park. This tour is the best way to experience the city of Bozeman.

Difficulty:
2 hours
10:00am every Sunday & Monday,
April - September
$20 per adult, $10 for youth
Kids 12 and under ride free

Rail-Trail History Tour

Your adventure guide will tell you all about the railway and Bozeman history as you explore the trails from Gallagator Linear Trail to Highland Glen Nature Preserve. The Gallagator now marks what once was an old railway line that extended all the way to Gallatin Gateway, and there’s lots to learn on the trail. This ride connects from the Gallagator to trails south of Kagy and Peets Hill, and wraps up with a picnic on the Highland Glen Nature Preserve to the east.

Difficulty:
2.5 hours
10:00am every Saturday,
April - September
$30 per adult, $15 for youth
Kids 12 and under ride free

Headwaters Trail Nature Tour Tour

This scenic stretch of smooth singletrack is the perfect family outing on a Saturday morning. Your adventure guide will point out the local plant and wildlife that flourish along the Missouri River. We’ll wrap up the tour with a picnic in one of the most scenic, hidden meadows along the headwaters.

Difficulty:
3 hours
10:00am every Saturday,
April - September
$40 per adult, $20 for youth
Kids 12 and under ride free

Green Mountain Ride

Just east of Bozeman is one of Montana’s most scenic treasures that you’ll have to see to believe. Our Green Mountain tour is the kind of classic trail ride that’ll keep you coming back for more. On this ride you’ll experience the great topographic diversity that Montana has to offer, and celebrate your ride to the top with a picnic and a 5-star view.

Difficulty:
5 hours
8:00am every Saturday,
May - August
$50 per adult, $30 for youth
Kids 12 and under ride free

Chestnut Mountain Thrill-Trail

This is a ride for experienced bikers looking for some real adventure. Chestnut Mountain features dramatic scenery and challenging, rocky trails that are a mountain biker’s dream. Just be careful that you aren’t swept off your feet– or at least, not off your bike! Our adventure guide will lead you safely through the most thrilling, scenic trails on Chestnut. Wrap up your heart-pumping ride with a picnic on the slopes of the mountain.

Difficulty:
5 hours
8:00am every Sunday,
May - August
$50 per adult, $30 for youth
Not recommended for beginners
