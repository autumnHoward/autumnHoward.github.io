Overview

Site Purpose

This website is a portfolio of my work. It is a place where I can showcase what I'm working on and the new skills that I learn. On this site, I will share relevant information about myself, original artwork, and links to pages that I design and build.

Target Audience

My target audience consists of potential employers or clients. This group represents a very diverse demographic with diverse needs, but all share a common need: a website or illustration. Since I wear several different "hats" and have a few different services I offer, consistency and clear communication through a strong visual hierarchy will be important across my site.

Typography

I want my site to have a creative and consistent feel. As a small portfolio site, I can get away with using a display style typeface for the headings. I want to pair the fun, artsy heading font with a clean, simple body font in order to create contrast and maximize legibility.
