Art Gallery

My first love in life was art. I studied Visual Arts with an emphasis in Illustration at Brigham Young University. I primarily draw and paint for personal enjoyment, though I often utilize digital illustration in my web ventures. If you're more interested in examples of my digital illustration work, check out the design page.

This page features some of my pieces from my school days at BYU. I work in several different media, including paint (oils, acrylics, gouache, and watercolor) charcoal, pastels, and print-making.
